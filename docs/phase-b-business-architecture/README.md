# Phase B: Business Architecture

## Overview

Phase B defines the business architecture that will support the enterprise hybrid cloud initiative. This phase establishes the business capability map, processes, and organizational structures needed to achieve the architecture vision.

## Objectives

- Define the business capability model
- Document key business processes
- Identify business services and their interactions
- Establish alignment between business and technology

## Documents in This Phase

| Document | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| [Capability Map](capability-map.md) | Business capability model and maturity assessment |
| [Business Processes](business-processes.md) | Key business process flows |

## Key Deliverables

1. **Business Capability Model** - Hierarchical view of business capabilities
2. **Business Process Catalog** - Documented business processes
3. **Organization Map** - Business units and their relationships
4. **Business Service Definitions** - Services provided by the business

## Capability Categories

| Category | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **Strategic** | Capabilities that differentiate the business | Innovation, Strategic Planning |
| **Core** | Capabilities that deliver primary value | Product Development, Customer Service |
| **Support** | Capabilities that enable core functions | HR, Finance, Legal |
| **Enabling** | Capabilities that enable digital operations | IT Services, Data Management |
